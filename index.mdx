## Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Wallet">
    Visit [chat402.xyz](https://www.chat402.xyz) and connect your MetaMask or Phantom wallet. Custodial wallets are automatically created for you.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fund Your Wallet">
    Send USDC to your deposit address on Base or Solana. Deposits confirm in 1-2 minutes.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Chatting">
    Select an AI model and start chatting! Each request shows the exact cost in real-time.
  </Step>
</Steps>
