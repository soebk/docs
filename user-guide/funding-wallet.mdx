## How to Fund Your Wallet

Chat402 uses USDC cryptocurrency on Base (Ethereum L2) or Solana networks.

### Step 1: Get Your Deposit Address

1. Connect your wallet (MetaMask or Phantom)
2. Go to the **API Keys** page
3. Copy your deposit address for either:
   - **Ethereum (Base)**: For MetaMask users
   - **Solana**: For Phantom users

### Step 2: Send USDC

  <Card title="Base (Ethereum L2)" icon="ethereum">

  <Card title="Solana" icon="s">

    - Gas fees: ~$0.0001
  </Card>

### Where to Get USDC

**For Base (Ethereum):**
- Bridge from Ethereum: [bridge.base.org](https://bridge.base.org)
- Buy on Coinbase
- Use Uniswap on Base

**For Solana:**
- Use Jupiter Exchange: [jup.ag](https://jup.ag)
- Buy in Phantom wallet
- Bridge from other chains

### Verification

Your balance will update automatically once the transaction confirms. Check your balance in:
- The sidebar (combined balance)
- The API Keys page (per-chain breakdown)
- The Chat page header
