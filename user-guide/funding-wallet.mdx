## How to Fund Your Wallet

Chat402 uses USDC cryptocurrency on the Solana network.

### Step 1: Get Your Deposit Address

1. Connect your wallet (Phantom or Solflare)
2. Go to the **API Keys** page
3. Copy your Solana deposit address

### Step 2: Send USDC

<Card title="Solana" icon="s">
  - Network: **Solana Mainnet**
  - Token: **USDC**
  - Confirmation: ~30 seconds
  - Gas fees: ~$0.0001
</Card>

### Where to Get USDC

**For Solana:**
- Use Jupiter Exchange: [jup.ag](https://jup.ag)
- Buy in Phantom wallet
- Bridge from other chains using [Portal Bridge](https://portalbridge.com)

### Verification

Your balance will update automatically once the transaction confirms. Check your balance in:
- The sidebar
- The API Keys page
- The Chat page header
